Who We Are

One band,
two musical worlds

Dhol Vocals Instruments Fusion

We are the only South-Asian fusion band at Rutgers, spreading culture by mixing South-Asian and Western styles through our talented vocalists, instrumentalists, and our founding instrument, the Dhol.

Our purpose is to re-imagine Desi musical culture in a modern, Western context — cultivating appreciation for the regional music of South Asia while giving student members a network to refine their musical abilities through mentorship.
