About RUDE

We are the only South-Asian Fusion Band at Rutgers, spreading culture by mixing South-Asian and Western styles through our talented vocalists, instrumentalists, and our founding instrument, the Dhol. Our purpose is to re-imagine Desi musical culture in a modern, Western context. We strive to cultivate appreciation and awareness for the regional music of South Asia, while also providing a network for student members to refine their musical abilities through mentorship.
