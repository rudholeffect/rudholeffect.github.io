About RUDE

Our mission, our sound, and the community behind it.

Our Mission

Rooted in tradition,
played in the present

We are the only South-Asian fusion band at Rutgers, spreading culture by mixing South-Asian and Western styles through our talented vocalists, instrumentalists, and our founding instrument, the Dhol.

Our purpose is to re-imagine Desi musical culture in a modern, Western context. We strive to cultivate appreciation and awareness for the regional music of South Asia, while providing a network for student members to refine their musical abilities through mentorship.

What We Do

Music, culture & community

01

Performance

From coffee houses to dance showcases, we perform at cultural events, competitions, and campus celebrations across Rutgers and beyond.

02

Fusion Sound

We blend contemporary and classical Indian styles with Western genres, creating arrangements that are rooted in tradition yet modern.

03

Community

RUDE is more than a band — it's a network. Members grow as musicians and leaders through mentorship and shared love for the art.

04

Cultural Outreach

We partner with South Asian student orgs and campus events to spread awareness of South Asian music traditions in a university setting.
