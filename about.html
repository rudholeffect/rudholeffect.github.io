About RUDE

Our mission, our sound, our story

Our Mission

Who We Are

We are the only South-Asian Fusion Band at Rutgers, spreading culture by mixing South-Asian and Western styles through our talented vocalists, instrumentalists, and our founding instrument, the Dhol. Our purpose is to re-imagine Desi musical culture in a modern, Western context. We strive to cultivate appreciation and awareness for the regional music of South Asia, while also providing a network for student members to refine their musical abilities through mentorship.

What We Do

Music, Culture & Community

Performance

From high-energy dhol showcases to soulful vocal sets, we perform at cultural events, competitions, and campus celebrations across Rutgers and beyond.

Fusion Sound

We blend Bollywood, Punjabi folk, and regional South Asian styles with Western genres — creating arrangements that are both rooted and fresh.

Community

RUDE is more than a band — it's a network. Members grow as musicians and leaders through peer mentorship, workshops, and shared love for the craft.

Cultural Outreach

We partner with South Asian student orgs and campus events to spread awareness of regional South Asian music traditions in a university setting.
